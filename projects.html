Virtual Taylor Frame

An interactive grid application designed to simulate a physical Taylor Frame, commonly used in mathematics education for visually impaired students. Digital, accessible, and audible.

Key Features

  • 18x25 interactive grid with resize options
  • Full keyboard navigation & shortcuts
  • High-fidelity text-to-speech feedback (cytolk)
  • Auditory cues for spatial awareness
